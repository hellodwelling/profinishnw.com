About Us

Pro Finish Painting & Construction LLC is a full-service Construction & Remodeling company serving Spokane, WA and surrounding areas. What we are best at Our Services

  • Home Construction
    Home Construction

    Our Home Construction service provides homeowners with a comprehensive, custom homebuilding experience. From design and planning to construction and finishing, we handle every step of the process to ensure your new home is perfect for you.

  • Kitchen Renovation
    Kitchen Renovation

    Kitchen renovations can be a great way to update the look and feel of your home. Our team can help you design a kitchen that fits your needs and budget. We'll take care of everything from demolition to installation, so you can enjoy your new kitchen in no time!

  • Bathroom Renovation
    Bathroom Renovation

    Our company offers a full range of construction and remodeling services, including bathroom renovations. We can help you design and build a new bathroom or renovate your current one. Our team is experienced and skilled in all aspects of bathroom renovation, from demolition to installation of fixtures and finishes.

  • Carpentry
    Carpentry

    Our Carpentry service provides high-quality woodworking and carpentry services for homeowners. We can help you with a wide range of projects, from building a new deck to installing trim around your windows. We have the experience and expertise to get the job done right, on time and on budget.

  • Deck & Patio Installation
    Deck & Patio Installation

    Our Deck & Patio Installation service offers a variety of design and construction options to create the perfect outdoor living space for your home. From decks and patios to pergolas and gazebos, we'll work with you to create the perfect addition that will add value and beauty to your home.

  • Flooring Installation
    Flooring Installation

    Our Flooring Installation service provides a quality, affordable flooring installation for your home. We have a wide variety of flooring to choose from and our installers are experienced professionals.
